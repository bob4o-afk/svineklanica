# Pitch Template — Fill in the Blanks

> A ready skeleton to fill in once your idea is built. Prepping the *structure* now is fully allowed (it's not code). The deep "how" is in `05_pitch_playbook.md`; this is the **fill-in-the-blank version** you complete Sunday morning. Format: **10 min pitch + 5 min Q&A**, Bulgarian-focused.
>
> Reminder of the scoring: Presentation/"Roast" 20% **and** it carries Radical Thinking 30%. The pitch is a deliverable — budget Sunday AM for it.

---

## The one-screen plan (fill these 5 lines first)
- **THE WOUND (cold open, 1 sentence):** ________________________________________
- **THE SYSTEMIC PROBLEM (not the symptom):** ____________________________________
- **THE DEMO'S DAMNING MOMENT (the punch):** _____________________________________
- **WHAT A CITIZEN DOES MONDAY (call to action):** ________________________________
- **THE BG REFERENCE / ROAST LINE:** ____________________ (e.g. close on *"Мутри вън."*)

---

## Slide skeleton (black bg, monospace, one idea/slide)
1. **[Cold open]** — just the wound, big type. No team intro. *(0:00–0:45)*
2. **[The problem]** — the systemic failure named plainly, in Bulgarian. *(0:45–2:00)*
3. **[Stakes]** — "this is your money / your vote / your news." *(2:00–2:45)*
4. **[LIVE DEMO]** — switch to the app, real BG data. *(2:45–6:30)*
5. **[The punch]** — freeze on the single most damning output. Dwell. *(6:30–7:30)*
6. **[It's real + yours]** — real data source, MIT open-source, runs local. *(7:30–8:30)*
7. **[Call to action]** — what a journalist/citizen does with it. *(8:30–9:00)*
8. **[Close]** — the BG roast line. Silence. *(9:00)*

*(Keep ≤9:00 rehearsed; leave buffer. The tool is the deck — minimize bullet slides.)*

## Cold-open fill-in (write 3 versions, pick the sharpest)
> Formula: **[a number / contradiction] + [pause] + [here they are].**
- v1: "________ . [pause] ________."
- v2: "________ . [pause] ________."
- v3: "________ . [pause] ________."

Example shape: *"The math says these 47 polling stations are statistically impossible. [pause] We didn't pick them — the data did."*

---

## The "roast" line bank (fill with YOUR facts)
- Deadpan fact: "________________ (state the absurd number flatly)."
- Juxtaposition: "They say ‘________’. The data says ‘________’."
- Name-the-pattern (not the person): "We won't name them — the [US Treasury / court / register] already did."
- Callback to cold open: "________________."
- BG punk close: *"Мутри вън."* / *"Системата разчита на твоето мълчание. Бъди шумен."*

---

## Demo script (rehearse to muscle memory — one clean path)
1. Start screen: ________________ (what's on screen at 2:45)
2. The one action: ________________ (the single click/input that triggers the reveal)
3. The reveal: ________________ (what appears — the punch)
4. The receipt: click through to ________________ (the real public source URL)
- [ ] **Backup screen-recording saved locally** (Wi-Fi insurance)

---

## Q&A index card (rehearse cold — print it)
| If they ask… | Answer |
|---|---|
| "Is the data real / source?" | "________ — public, open re-use. [URL ready]" |
| "Isn't this just an anomaly, not proof?" | "Correct — we flag, we don't convict. We hand [journalists] a sorted list of where to look." |
| "Aren't you defaming people?" | "Only public records + officially-designated figures; every claim links to source." |
| "What's new vs [existing tool]?" | "Credit them, then our delta: ________ (live / local / usable-by-anyone / open-source)." |
| "Does it actually run?" | "Yes — pick any value, I'll run it live now." |
| "Could it be abused for disinfo?" | "Sourced + factual + no de-anonymizing redacted data. Punk is fact-based." |

---

## Roles for the 15 minutes (3 people)
- **Voice:** cold open, problem, punch, close. The sharpest talker.
- **Driver:** runs the live demo, knows every keystroke + the fallback recording.
- **Defender:** owns data provenance + answers hostile Q&A (every source URL memorized).
- [ ] Full run rehearsed **twice**, timed. Cut anything that isn't the wound, the demo, or the punch.

---

## Final pre-pitch checklist
- [ ] Cold open true + sourced  · [ ] Demo on real BG data, one clean path  · [ ] Backup recording
- [ ] Punch identified + rehearsed  · [ ] Every claim has a source URL  · [ ] Repo public + MIT
- [ ] Runs offline  · [ ] ≤9:00 with silence  · [ ] One BG punk line woven in  · [ ] Q&A card memorized
